Find Yourself

A Study Partner

Its been proven time and time again that studying in a group is a fantastic way of improving grades, increasing retention and boosting our memory. Working in a group and learning together is an extremely effective way to understand complex subjects better.


However, finding a group of people to form that study group is not a easy or simple task. Its also extremely difficult to find partners for a group project etc. This tool aims to at least reduce that difficulty if not completely remove it.


The tool is simple to use,


  1. Enter the relevant info regarding yourself such as Degree, Units, Schedule, Major etc
  2. The system then analyses each entry in our student database and assigns points indicating how suitable a student is for you. For Example: If you both have the same Major then a point is assigned to the student, if in the same unit then another point is assigned etc etc.
  3. The students who then have the greatest points are provided to you in a list so you can contact them.

Its similar to a dating system, a way for two student who have the most common with each other to find each other and work together.


The tool also allows you to indicate if you are looking for a project partner of a certain unit and students in the same unit are prioritised for you.
